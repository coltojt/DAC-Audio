About

My name is Colton Tucker and I am the crafter and user-owner of Digital Analagoue Creations in Tulsa, Oklahoma. I am nothing short of obsessed with vacuum tube amplifier and analog audio technology and seek to preserve this vanishing art for future generations of audiophiles. I specialize in handwired, point-to-point vacuum tube amplifiers; crafted with precision, tested thoroughly, built to sing and to last a lifetime. With a focus on authentic tone, quality, and classic audio character, each of my creations reflect old-school craftsmanship and a deep appreciation for analog sound.

I also offer repair and restoration services for vacuum tube amplifiers and audio electronics equipment as well as thorough guitar setups on a case-by-case basis. Please don't hesitate to contact me with your needs, I am always happy to point patrons in the right direction.
