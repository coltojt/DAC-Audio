About

My name is Colton Tucker, and I am the craftsman and technician behind DAC Audio in Tulsa, Oklahoma. I’m obsessed with vacuum tube amplification and analog audio technology—an art that’s fading fast. My work is dedicated to preserving it.

I build hand-wired, point-to-point vacuum tube amplifiers with precision, intention, and respect for classic audio engineering. Every amp is tested thoroughly, voiced carefully, and built to sing for a lifetime.

Tube Amp Repair in Tulsa

I also provide diagnostic, repair, and restoration services for vacuum tube amplifiers and select audio equipment and electronics here in Tulsa, Oklahoma. Whether your amp is noisy, weak, cutting out, or ready for a full overhaul, I offer careful bench work with a focus on tone, reliability, and longevity.

  • Tube amp troubleshooting and diagnostics
  • Capacitor, resistor, and component replacement
  • Vintage Fender, Marshall, and Vox amplifier restoration
  • Tube biasing and performance checks
  • Custom hand-wired amplifier builds and modifications

If you're in the Tulsa area and need tube amp repair or restoration, feel free to reach out through the contact page. All work is done locally and by appointment. If you need guidance or aren’t sure where to start, feel free to reach out—I’m always glad to help or point you in the right direction.
