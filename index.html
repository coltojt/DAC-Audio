About Us

My name is Colton Tucker and I am a lone creator, crafter,and owner of DAC Audio in Tulsa, Oklahoma. I am currently developing my first custom line of Handwired, Point-to-Point Vacuum Tube Amplifiers. With a focus on tone, reliability, and classic audio character, each build reflects a deep love for analog sound and vintage-modern aesthetic.

I also offer repair and troubleshooting services for tube amplifiers and audio electronics equipment on a case-by-case basis. From diagnostics to fine-tuning, I bring old-school craftsmanship and attention to detail to every piece we service.
