Services & Pricing

  • Free Email Consultation: Send me a pic and/or name-model number of your equipment as well as a description of the issue; I'll let you know if it's a project I can handle up front.
  • Troubleshooting & Diagnostics: $50 flat rate (applied toward final cost if repaired).
  • Complete Recap Service: Starting at $250+. Includes Replacement of all aging electrolytic capacitors using premium components. Restores stability, reduces hum, and ensures safe, reliable operation. Price varies by model and accessibility.
  • Basic Tube Amp Biasing: $40 flat rate if chassis already open or easily accessible. Adjust existing tubes and verify idle current. Includes replacement but not cost of tubes.
  • Bias + Chassis Removal: $75-90 to remove/reinstall chassis, bias tubes, inspect and verify plate voltage and bias current.
  • Power Cord Conversion: $100 including parts and labor. Replace 2 prong with grounded, 3 prong power cord. This is absolutely essential for safety in vintage equipment.
  • Repairs & Modifications: Price based on complexity and parts needed (by consultation).
  • Guitar Pickup Replacement: $40 per single, $70 per pair, $90 per trio.
  • No job is too small: Contact me to inquire about a service not on this list, i.e. scratchy or faulty input jacks, poor solder connections, etc.
