General Policies

  • Minimum bench/estimate fee is required at drop-off. (See Services page for details.)
  • Unclaimed items: Equipment not picked up within 30 days of service completion (or declined service) will incur a $5/day storage fee for up to 60 days. After 60 days, items are considered abandoned and may be sold or disposed of by Digital Analogue Creations.
  • Storage fees cannot be waived once applied.
  • Outstanding invoices may be sent to collections. If small claims court is required, legal fees and lost wages will be added to the balance.
  • Equipment left more than 60 days after repair completion with an unpaid balance becomes property of Digital Analogue Creations. To surrender an item, request and complete a surrender form (available in person or via email). Diagnosis fees still apply.
  • Repairs over $100 in parts require full prepayment of parts and shipping before repair begins. Labor is due at pickup.
  • Returned checks/e-checks will incur a $90 service fee.
