About Us

We specialize in handwired, point-to-point vacuum tube amplifiers — crafted with precision, tested thoroughly, and built to sing. With a focus on tone, quality, and classic audio character, each build reflects years of experience and a deep love for analog sound.

We also offer repair and restoration services for tube amplifiers and audio electronics equipment on a case-by-case basis.
